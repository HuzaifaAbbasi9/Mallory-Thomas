WHAT I OFFER

User Experience design is the commitment to designing procucts that are useful, easy to use, enjoyable to interact with and are created with the customer in mind. It's about enhancing the experience that people have while using your procuct and making sure they finc value in what you provice.

A brand identity is made up of what your brand says, what your values are, how you communicate your product and what you want people to feel when they interact with it. Essentially, your brand identity is the personality of your business and a promise to your customers. A brand is what makes you memorable.

Visual design shapes and improves the user experience by focusing on color, images, fonts and other visual elements.
